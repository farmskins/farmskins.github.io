TOP 5 donk’s CS2 Skins

farmskins

It's no secret that donk has already become a star in CS2 at just 17 years old, with enormous potential, and is already vying for the title of the top player in the world. Not long ago, we thoroughly examined all of donk's settings in CS2, and we don't want to stop there. In this article, we'll analyze donk from the perspective of his CS2 skins, look at which skins he prioritizes, how much they cost, and why donk has chosen these particular skins.

Donk's Config in CS2

donk's Knives in CS2

Donk plays with two knives, one for the CT side and one for the T side, and it's clear he has a preference for a certain skin, namely Doppler.

Butterfly Knife | Doppler – This CS2 knife features an exquisite pattern and shimmering texture. This skin adds an element of style and elegance to every twist and turn in the game. Many esports players choose Doppler not only for its high price and rarity but also for its incredible beauty.

Price: $2800

Cases: Knives, Covertknives

M9 Bayonet | Doppler – Similar in design to the Butterfly Knife but with a more massive and menacing look, making it an excellent choice for those who want to emphasize their strength on the battlefield. The M9 Bayonet | Doppler embodies donk's aggressive and powerful playstyle!

Price: $1615

Cases: Knives, Pixel Future, Blaze, Doppy

donk's Gloves in CS2

Like with knives, donk uses different gloves for the CT and T sides. It’s noticeable that donk always opts for bright skins. In this case, the esports player chooses Sport Gloves | Hedge Maze and Sport Gloves | Amphibious.

Sport Gloves | Hedge Maze – These gloves feature green and black patterns reminiscent of a hedge maze. They give the player an elegant and fresh look, perfectly matching donk's various CS2 skins.

Price: $3999

Cases: Gungnir, Gloves

Sport Gloves | Amphibious – These gloves have a bluish pattern reminiscent of aquatic motifs. They add style and uniqueness to the player's appearance, highlighting attention to detail.

Price: $1685

Cases: Gungnir, Gloves, Clutch Case

donk's AWP in CS2

Although the AWP isn't donk's primary weapon, he does occasionally use it, such as on the Dust 2 map. And of course, donk has chosen AWP | Dragon Lore as his main skin.

AWP | Dragon Lore – This is one of the rarest and most expensive CS2 skins in the game. It features a unique design with a dragon and rich colors, making it the dream of many players.

Price: $11675

Cases: Gungnir, Exclusive, AWP, Lore

donk's Rifles in CS2

Probably what everyone has been waiting for: donk's primary CS2 weapons. These are the weapons he gets most of his kills with, and they are a big part of his popularity. The AK-47 | Bloodsport and M4A1-S | Hot Rod are in donk's arsenal.

M4A1-S | Hot Rod – This skin stands out with its bright and sleek red coating. It's perfect for players who prefer an aggressive playstyle and want their weapon to look the part, which perfectly fits our hero today, doesn't it?

Price: $1000

Cases: Gungnir, Exclusive, Classified, M4A4 & M4A1-S, The Boss, M4A1 Silence

AK-47 | Bloodsport – This skin features a rich and intricate design, reminiscent of sports cars and racing motorcycles. It symbolizes speed and power, making it a popular choice among players.

Price: $130

Cases: Exclusive, Covert, AK-47, Blaze, The Boss, Gunsmith, Ez Covert, Lucky AK-47, Lotus

donk's Pistols in CS2

CS2 pistols for donk are as much of a signature as his rifles. Sometimes, you can't decide whether you’re more amazed by donk’s one-taps or the beauty of his skins. USP-S | Kill Confirmed, Glock-18 | Dragon Tattoo, and Desert Eagle | Golden Koi are the choices of a true professional.

USP-S | Kill Confirmed – This skin has a bright and memorable design featuring comic book motifs. It adds an element of fun and brightness to every shootout.

Price: $142

Cases: Gungnir, Exclusive, Covert, Covertgloves, Top Guns, The Boss, Ez Covert

Glock-18 | Dragon Tattoo – This skin boasts a refined design featuring a dragon. It adds class and style to the standard pistol, making it visually appealing.

Price: $100

Desert Eagle | Golden Koi – This skin features a bright golden coating with patterns reminiscent of koi carp. It symbolizes wealth and sophistication, making it a coveted item among players.

Price: $140

Cases: Gunsmith, Recoil

Other donk’s CS2 Skins

As a bonus, we decided to show you skins that donk doesn't use as often but not mentioning them would be a crime. Just look at these skins: M4A4 | Buzz Kill, FAMAS | Rapid Eye Movement, Galil AR | Chromatic Aberration, MP7 | Fade.

M4A4 | Buzz Kill – This skin has a bright and aggressive design in yellow and black tones. It’s perfect for those who want to emphasize their dominant position on the battlefield.

Price: $30

Cases: Covertknives, Covert, M4A4 & M4A1-S, StatTrak Abuse

FAMAS | Rapid Eye Movement – This skin stands out with its bright color accents and psychedelic pattern. It attracts attention and adds an element of extravagance to the player's arsenal.

Price: $8

Cases: Pixel Future, Aladdin, Dreams & Nightmares

Galil AR | Chromatic Aberration – This skin features a futuristic and iridescent design that looks straight out of science fiction. It adds a unique visual effect, making the weapon stand out among others.

Price: $9

Cases: Sand

MP7 | Fade – This skin features a smooth and iridescent color transition from gold to purple. It gives the weapon an elegant and refined look, making it a popular choice among players.

Price: $6

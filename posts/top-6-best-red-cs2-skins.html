Top 6 Best Red CS2 Skins

farmskins

Skins in CS2 have long been inseparable from the gameplay itself. It’s hard to imagine any esports player or regular player without CS2 skins. But how to stand out the most among the mass of skins? Our choice is red CS2 skins. They are bright, combative, and the most noticeable. In this article, Farmskins has compiled the top 6 best red CS2 skins for you.

AK-47 | Bloodsport

The AK-47 | Bloodsport first appeared in the game in 2017 in the Spectrum Case and was immediately loved by many players. Its design references futuristic Japanese culture, with many comparing this skin to the iconic anime Akira, and it’s hard to disagree with that.

The CS2 skin is painted in red, black, and white colors. Stickers with numbers and patterns are applied to the surface of the weapon, giving it the look of a sports car. It’s no wonder this skin remains one of the most popular in CS2 history.

It's also worth noting that as the CS2 AK-47 | Bloodsport wears, it only gets darker, with the patterns and inscriptions remaining almost untouched, which is another advantage of this skin.

M4A4 | Howl

The M4A4 | Howl is a legendary CS2 skin that needs no introduction. It is the only contraband skin of its kind, likely to remain so. The M4A4 | Howl appeared in the game in 2014 with the Huntsman Weapon Case.

The CS2 M4A4 | Howl is the most expensive skin in the game, and here’s why. Back in 2014, it was discovered that the artwork on the skin was stolen, so the design was quickly redone, and the skin was removed from the cases.

This skin is done in dark red tones, with a howling wolf depicted on the weapon. Although not everyone can afford this skin, every self-respecting collector must have it in their collection.

AWP | Wildfire

The AWP | Wildfire appeared in the game in October 2019 in the CS20 collection. It was a real gift for all fans of fiery CS2 skins.

The AWP | Wildfire is made in red, yellow, and dark colors. A dragon is depicted on the weapon's surface, breathing fire and melting the hearts of all CS2 fans.

USP-S | Kill Confirmed

The USP-S | Kill Confirmed first appeared in the game in September 2015 and remains, in our opinion, the most beautiful USP-S skin to this day.

Just look at this design. Blood and flames pass through a shot skull. Very bright and yet not flashy. In a word - brutal!

M4A1-S | Chantico's Fire

Let's add some luxury to our modest selection with the CS2 M4A1-S | Chantico's Fire. The skin first appeared in August 2016 in the Gamma 2 Case.

The M4A1-S | Chantico's Fire is a truly royal skin in red and gold colors. Stained glass windows are depicted all over the skin’s surface. To us, this skin resembles Fabergé eggs displayed in royal palaces or museums.

AK-47 | The Empress

Tarot and CS2 is a nuclear mix. That’s exactly what the creators of the AK-47 | The Empress wanted to demonstrate. This CS2 skin first appeared in September 2017 in the Spectrum 2 Case along with other skins from the Tarot collection.

The AK-47 | The Empress depicts an empress in a combination of red and gold colors, giving a majestic look to the entire skin composition. The small details in the Tarot style give the AK-47 | The Empress a mystical appearance.

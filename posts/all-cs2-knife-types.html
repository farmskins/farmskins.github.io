All CS2 Knife Types

The knife in CS2 is a universal weapon available to both sides. It has always been a distinctive symbol associated with success, coolness, skill, wealth, and all other benefits of humanity. Owning a CS2 knife is a sign of high class. Cheap knives cost around $100, but the most expensive ones with rare float values can be priced at several thousand dollars, sometimes even tens of thousands.

Bayonet 

The Bayonet was introduced in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive with the Arms Deal update in August 2013. It can be found in various cases such as the Operation Bravo and Chroma 2 cases. The Bayonet's design is inspired by the real M9 Bayonet, widely used by the US military since the 1980s.

Bowie Knife 

The Bowie Knife was added to CS:GO during the Operation Wildfire update in February 2016. It is available in the Operation Wildfire Case. This knife is based on the real Bowie knife, characterized by a large clip-point blade, made famous by 19th-century American pioneer Jim Bowie.

Butterfly Knife 

The Butterfly Knife in CS2 was introduced with the Operation Breakout update in July 2014 and can be found in the Operation Breakout Case. Its design reflects the real balisong or butterfly knife, known for its folding blade and handles popular for performing tricks and maneuvers.

Classic Knife 

The Classic Knife was added to CS:GO with the introduction of Operation Shattered Web in November 2019. It can be found in the Shattered Web Case. This knife pays homage to the original knife model from early Counter-Strike games, adding a touch of nostalgia for longtime players.

Falchion Knife 

The Falchion CS2 Knife was introduced with the Falchion Case in May 2015 during the Operation Bloodhound update. Its design is inspired by the real falchion sword, a single-edged European sword from the medieval period.

Flip Knife

 

The Flip Knife was added to CS:GO  with the Arms Deal 2 update in November 2013 and can be found in various cases including the eSports 2013 Winter and Chroma cases. Its design features a modern tactical folding blade, popular among outdoor enthusiasts and tactical users.

Gut Knife 

The Gut Knife was introduced with the Arms Deal update in August 2013. It is available in cases such as the eSports 2013 and Winter Offensive cases. Its design includes a gut hook on the back of the blade, often used in hunting for field dressing game.

Huntsman Knife

The Huntsman Knife was added during the Operation Phoenix update in May 2014 and can be found in the Huntsman Case. It is modeled after real tactical survival knives, known for their rugged construction and versatile use in outdoor and combat situations.

Karambit 

The CS2 Karambit knife was introduced in CS:GO with the Arms Deal update in August 2013. This knife can be found in various cases including the Operation Phoenix and Chroma cases. Its design is inspired by the traditional Southeast Asian karambit, known for its curved blade and ring handle.

Kukri Knife

The Kukri Knife was added to CS2 in the Kilowatt Case and is known for its unique curved blade inspired by the traditional Nepalese kukri used by Gurkhas. Its real-life prototype is famed for its versatility in combat and everyday tasks.

M9 Bayonet 

The M9 Bayonet was introduced with the Arms Deal update in August 2013. It can be found in cases such as the Operation Bravo and Chroma 2 cases. The M9 Bayonet is a multifunctional knife used by the military, capable of attaching to rifles as a bayonet.

Navaja Knife 

The Navaja Knife was added to CS:GO during the Horizon update in August 2018 and found in the Horizon Case. Its design is based on the traditional Spanish folding knife, known for its slim blade and historical significance in Spanish culture.

Nomad Knife 

The Nomad CS2 Knife was introduced during the Shattered Web update in November 2019 and can be found in the Shattered Web Case. Its design is inspired by modern survival knives, known for their durability and adaptability in various outdoor conditions.

Paracord Knife 

The Paracord Knife was added to CS:GO with the Operation Shattered Web update in November 2019 and is available in the Shattered Web Case. Its design includes a minimalist blade with a paracord-wrapped handle, similar to real tactical knives used by the military and survivalists.

Shadow Daggers 

The Shadow Daggers were introduced with the Shadow Case in September 2015 during the Shadow Boxing update. Their unique design features paired knives inspired by push daggers used in close combat.

Skeleton Knife 

The Skeleton Knife was added to CS:GO during the Shattered Web update in November 2019 and can be found in the Shattered Web Case. Its design includes a skeletonized handle and blade, reducing weight while maintaining functionality, similar to real tactical knives.

Stiletto Knife 

The CS2 Stiletto Knife was introduced with the Horizon update in August 2018 and found in the Horizon Case. It is based on the real stiletto, known for its thin blade and ease of concealed carry, often associated with historical Italian weaponry.

Survival Knife 

The Survival Knife was added during the Shattered Web update in November 2019 and found in the Shattered Web Case. Its design is inspired by modern survival knives, designed for durability and versatility in harsh conditions.

Talon Knife 

The Talon Knife was introduced with the Horizon update in August 2018 and can be found in the Horizon Case. It is modeled after the real karambit, featuring a curved blade and ergonomic handle for effective slicing and chopping motions.

Ursus Knife 

The Ursus Knife was added to CS:GO during the Horizon update in August 2018 and is available in the Horizon Case. Its design includes a sturdy construction with a tanto-style blade, inspired by modern tactical knives used for utility and combat purposes.

Conclusion 

Knives in Counter-Strike 2 play a significant role not only as weapons but also as collectible items and status symbols among players. Each knife presented has a unique design inspired by real-life prototypes, adding depth and variety to the gameplay. From the classic Bayonet to the unique Talon Knife, each CS2 knife type has its history and features that can influence a player's style and preferences. With regular updates and new additions, such as the left hand for the Bowie Knife, Valve continues to maintain interest in the game and enhance the player experience. Choosing a knife becomes not only a matter of aesthetics but also of strategy, adding another layer of tactical depth to the exciting world of Counter-Strike 2.

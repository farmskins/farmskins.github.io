5 Key Mechanics of Deadlock Every Player Should Know

In the world of gaming, there's always room to learn new mechanics, even if you've spent hundreds of hours in Deadlock. This game, often regarded as a successor to Dota 2, offers unique opportunities for players. Here are five key Deadlock mechanics that can significantly increase your chances of victory.

Movement

One of the main differences in Deadlock is the ability for vertical movement. Make the most of this feature: to reduce your hitbox, crouch during shootouts or perform rolls. To save stamina, use the key combinations of shift, ctrl, and space for dodges and double jumps. Don’t forget to practice in the lobby to hone your skills.

Right-Side View

This mechanic allows you to build your positioning in battle. When you’re behind an obstacle and aim at an enemy, your character can peek out from cover while the rest of their model remains hidden. This significantly reduces the likelihood of getting hit by bullets and makes it easier to farm creeps on the lane, as you'll be safer.

Creeps Skipping

Even though creeps in Deadlock travel along the zipline in special capsules, you can still destroy them. Simply shoot at the capsules, and the creeps will fall out. This allows you not only to farm a pack but also to shorten the enemy zipline, helping you capture territory.

Additional Sources of Souls

Don’t limit yourself to farming on the lane; explore other options for acquiring souls. You can:

  • Destroy forest creeps;

  • Break the soul machine;

  • Obtain souls from statuettes or crates;

  • Bring a Soul Urn to the altar.

These additional sources will help you quickly build up the necessary supply of souls to enhance your character.

Parrying

Parrying in Deadlock is an important mechanic that allows you to block melee attacks and stun the attacker in return. This function is set to the F key by default and works great against melee heroes. While the enemy is stunned, you will have the opportunity to distance yourself and take up a favorable position.

Conclusion

By knowing these basics, you can more easily integrate into the game. Pay attention to the map and plan your escape routes in advance. Good luck in your battles in Deadlock!

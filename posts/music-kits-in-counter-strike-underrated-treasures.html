Music Kits in Counter-Strike: Underrated Treasures

farmskins

Music kits in Counter-Strike (CS) often remain overshadowed by more popular skins and stickers, despite their vast potential. A well-chosen music kit can significantly enhance gameplay, making the experience more comfortable and enjoyable. While professional players typically disable music, leaving only the sounds of the bomb timer, music can be a great addition for casual modes and even enhance mood in competitive matches. In this article, we'll explore the variety of CS music kits, categorize them by genre, and help you select one that suits your musical tastes.

Best Sound and Audio Settings in CS2

Electronic Music

CS2 Music Kits:

  • Make U SWEAT! by Knock2 

  • Reason by Rad Cat 

  • Under Bright Lights by TWERL & Ekko & Sidetrack 

  • inhuman by ISOxo 

  • dashstar* by Knock2  

  • Vici by Freaky DNA  

  • Diamonds by Mord Fustang

  • I Am by AWOLNATION  

Electronic music is the most popular genre in CS2. These kits are perfect for EDM fans and dance music lovers. Each track energizes the game, with bass and rhythms making every win and loss more emotional. Special mention goes to I Am by AWOLNATION, featuring tracks popular in TikTok videos and the legendary "Run" meme.

Hard Rock

CS2 Music Kits:

  • Void by Humanity’s Last Breath  

  • Lock Me Up by Sullivan King  

  • FREE by Hundredth  

  • III-Arena by Skog  

  • II-Headshot by Skog  

Fans of heavy music, including trash metal, heavy metal, black metal, and hardcore, will appreciate these CS2 kits. They are filled with powerful instrumentals and emotional vocals, making them ideal for intense matches. III-Arena by Skog kit is especially suitable for newcomers, as it lacks vocals and is less aggressive.

80’s Synths

CS2 Music Kits:

  • Heading for the Source by 3kliksphilip  

  • Bodacious by Sam Marshall  

  • Hotline Miami by Various Artists 

  • Work Hard, Play Hard by Laura Shigihara  

This genre takes us back to the era of neon and synthesizers of the 80s. The music in these CS2 kits is perfect for retro sound fans and those who prefer minimal vocals. Hotline Miami by Various Artists  kit stands out with its soundtrack from the Hotline Miami.

Cinematic Style

CS2 Music Kits:

  • Astro Bellum by Jesse Harlin  

  • Neo Noir by Tim Huling  

  • Drifter by Matt Levine  

  • M.U.D.D. FORCE by Tree Adams & Ben Bromfield  

These CS2 kits create an atmosphere similar to movie soundtracks, adding emotional depth to the game. Regardless of the scenario on the map, the music from these CS2 kits will elevate your mood and help you focus on gameplay.

Hip-Hop

CS2 Music Kits:

  • Gothic Luxury bu Meechy Darko  

  • u mad! by bbno$  

  • ULTIMATE by Denzel Curry 

  • King, Scar by Scarlxrd  

These CS2 kits offer a unique approach to hip-hop, combining dynamic rhythms and emotional rap. The MVP sounds from these CS2 kits are often heard on servers, allowing you to get on your opponents' nerves and recharge after each MVP award.

Punk Rock

CS2 Music Kits:

  • The Lowlife Pack by Neck Deep  

  • Backbone by Roam  

  • Life’s Not Out To Get You by Neck Deep  

  • The Good Youth by Blitz Kids  

Punk rock lovers will appreciate these CS2 music kits for their nostalgic riffs in the style of Sum41, The Offspring, and Blink-182. While some kits may seem expensive, they add a unique sound to the game.

Ethnic Music

CS2 Music Kits:

  • KOLIBRI by Sarah Schachner  

  • Desert Fire by Austin Wintory  

  • Mocha Petal by Austin Wintory  

  • Gunman Taco Truck by Dren  

  • Bachram by Austin Wintory  

  • 花脸 Hua Lian (Painted Face) by Perfect World  

These CS2 music kits are surprising in their originality and diversity. Here you can find Scandinavian motifs, folk dances, Mexican indie sounds, and much more. 花脸 Hua Lian (Painted Face) by Perfect World, released in honor of the fifth anniversary of the Chinese version of Counter-Strike, is particularly noteworthy.

CS2 Music Kits Featuring Pro Players

CS2 Music Kits:

  • EZ4ENCE by The Verkkars 

  • Flashbang Dance by The Verkkars & n0thing  

These CS2 kits are unique, created with the participation of professional players. For example, EZ4ENCE by The Verkkars — features the former captain of ENCE, Aleksib, while Flashbang Dance was made with the help of American scene veteran n0thing.

Music kits in CS2 can greatly enrich your gaming experience, adding unique sound elements and creating the perfect atmosphere for each match. No matter your musical preferences, you'll find something to enjoy.

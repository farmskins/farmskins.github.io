New Sticker Mechanics in CS2

Valve made significant changes to how stickers work in CS2. Here are the key updates:

  • Stickers can now be placed freely, with the option to rotate them.

  • Each weapon now supports up to five stickers.

  • Players can zoom in on their weapons for more precise sticker application.

In CS:GO, sticker placement was limited to four fixed positions, but CS2 gives players complete freedom. Now, you can stick them anywhere on your weapon and even spell out words or your nickname.

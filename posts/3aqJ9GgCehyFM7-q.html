The Basics of Repo

Before diving into strategies, it’s important to understand how the game works. Repo is a team-based game where coordination and planning are crucial. You’ll need to interact with objects, avoid monsters, and manage limited resources like health and stamina. Each monster has unique behaviors, and learning their patterns is essential. The game rewards careful movement and smart teamwork over reckless rushing.

Stealth and Caution

Staying quiet and moving carefully are the best ways to survive. Running, talking in voice chat, or making noise can attract certain monsters. Move slowly, use cover, and have an escape plan in case something goes wrong. Some monsters are more sensitive to sound than others, so if you hear one nearby, stop and wait until it moves away.

Using the Map Effectively

The map is your best tool for navigation. It shows player locations, loot, purchased items, and evacuation points. Check it often to plan safe routes and avoid getting lost. Communicating with your team about the best paths can make a huge difference in surviving.

Evacuation: How to Escape Safely

To survive, you need to leave the level with your collected items. Make sure all loot is placed properly inside the evacuation zone. If anything falls outside, it will be lost when the extraction starts. Do not stand in the evacuation area while extraction is in progress, or you will die instantly. As soon as the final item is placed, start moving toward the exit since the lights will go out, making it harder to see.

Managing Items and Inventory

Not all items are the same. Fragile objects like cups and potions can break if dropped or hit. Carry them carefully and avoid collisions. Strength upgrades allow you to carry heavier items alone, making them a valuable investment. Be mindful of traps, as some objects might trigger alarms or attract monsters.

Upgrades and Purchases

Choosing the right upgrades early on can make the game much easier. Stamina upgrades let you run longer, health upgrades increase your survivability, and strength upgrades allow you to carry heavier items. Item prices increase after each purchase, so plan ahead and buy in bulk when possible.

Teamwork and Communication

Repo game is a cooperative game, and teamwork is key. Share resources when necessary, like transferring 10 health points to a teammate if they are low. Coordinate movements and loot collection to avoid wasting time. Secret areas, like the hidden stash of grenades in the store, can be accessed if teammates work together to lift someone up.

Dealing with Monsters

Each monster has its own behavior, but some general survival rules apply. Avoid making noise, stay out of their sight, and use distractions when necessary. Some creatures track movement, while others react only to sound. If a monster is chasing you, leading it into a trap or a dead-end area can buy your team time to escape. Running should be a last resort, as it drains stamina quickly and may alert more threats.

As you gain experience, you’ll learn more advanced strategies and improve your ability to navigate the game’s many challenges.

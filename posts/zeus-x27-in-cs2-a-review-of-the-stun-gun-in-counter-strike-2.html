Zeus x27 in CS2: A Review of the Stun Gun in Counter-Strike 2

Zeus x27 in Counter-Strike 2 is a weapon similar to a standard police stun gun. Although inexpensive, it has been an effective and popular choice for eliminating enemies across various versions of Counter-Strike, including CS 1.6 and CS:GO. This article delves into how CS2 gun - Zeus - has changed with the "Arms Race" update, the best strategies for using it, and tips on countering enemies who wield this weapon.

Description of Zeus x27

  • Alternative Names: Zeus, shocker, taser

  • Availability: Accessible to both Counter-Terrorists and Terrorists

  • Fire Mode: Single shot

  • Magazine Capacity: 1 shot, with infinite reloads

  • Reload Time: 30 seconds

  • Kill Range: 5 meters

  • Lethal Damage: Any part of the enemy’s body

  • Movement Speed: 220 units per second while equipped

  • Console Command: give weapon_taser

Key Features of Zeus x27:

  • Purpose: The Zeus x27 is primarily used for close-quarters combat in narrow spaces, where it allows players to eliminate enemies and take their weapons. This can potentially turn the tide of a round.

  • Damage: The standout feature of Zeus x27 is its ability to deal lethal damage upon a brief contact, killing the enemy instantly with a single hit. It deals 100% damage upon hitting the target's hitbox.

  • Range: While Zeus x27 is highly effective at short distances, its effectiveness dramatically decreases beyond a 5-meter range.

  • Cost: Priced at $200 in CS2, the Zeus is an affordable weapon for a guaranteed close-range kill.

  • Limitations: Zeus in CS2 no longer provides a kill reward. Instead of dropping after use, the weapon now has a 30-second reload time after each shot.

  • Uniqueness: Zeus is the only weapon in the game that guarantees damage on direct contact, making it a highly situational, yet powerful tool.

In a recent update, Zeus x27 received its first skin, and it is now available during matches in the “Arms Race” mode, awarded at every level except the final one.

Pros and Cons of Zeus x27

Pros:

  • Low price

  • Lethal damage at close range

  • Can shift momentum by securing better weapons from enemies

  • Increased movement speed when equipped

Cons:

  • Limited range (5 meters)

  • No reward for kills

  • One shot before needing a 30-second reload

  • Ineffective against multiple enemies in quick succession

How to Spawn Zeus in CS2

Zeus x27 is typically purchased through the gear section in the game’s buy menu. However, if you are in a custom server where you cannot access the buy menu, you can spawn Zeus using the following console command:

give weapon_taser.

To automate this process, you can bind Zeus x27 to a key by entering the following command:

bind "key" give weapon_taser.

Tactics for Using Zeus x27

  • Economic Situations: Zeus is an ideal choice when your team is low on money, and you need to secure a weapon from an opponent to use in the next round. However, keep in mind the 30-second reload time between shots.

  • Positioning: Use Zeus in close-range positions, such as the underpass or palace entrance on Mirage, or the hut and vents on Nuke. These areas force enemies into close combat, maximizing the effectiveness of the taser.

  • Pro Scene: Zeus has seen use in professional play, particularly in CS2 pistol rounds for Counter-Terrorists. For example, FaZe Clan’s Ropz frequently uses Zeus in pistol rounds, securing kills from unexpected angles.

Countering Zeus x27

To effectively play against opponents using Zeus x27, consider these tips:

  • Distance Control: Always be aware of your distance from opponents. Zeus users must close in to a 5-meter range for a kill, so stay at a distance or use cover to your advantage.

  • Grenades: Zeus users typically do not wear armor in eco rounds, making them vulnerable to well-placed grenades.

  • Teamwork: Play in pairs to trade kills. If an enemy with Zeus eliminates one teammate, you can quickly retaliate, as the Zeus user will be defenseless for 30 seconds after firing.

  • Positional Awareness: Zeus users rely on surprise attacks from tight spaces. Knowing common positions and using grenades to clear them can reduce their chances of success.

Final Thoughts on Zeus x27 in CS2

Zeus x27 is a unique and inexpensive weapon in CS2. With its ability to deal lethal damage on contact, it is a valuable tool for eco rounds and surprise plays. However, its short range and single shot limitation require players to carefully plan their movements. For those who master the use of Zeus, it can be a game-changer in critical moments.

By practicing with the Zeus x27 and understanding its strengths and limitations, players can unlock its full potential in matches. Though it may not be a weapon for every situation, when used strategically, it can provide a powerful advantage.

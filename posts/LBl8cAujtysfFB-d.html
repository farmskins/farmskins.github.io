What Are Binds in CS2?

Binds are custom key assignments that execute specific commands in the game. This allows you to perform actions instantly without opening menus or typing commands in the console.

If you're not sure how to create binds in CS2, simply use the command:

bind [key] "[action]"

For example:

bind "F" "use weapon_flashbang" — Now pressing F will instantly throw a flashbang. Simple and efficient.With custom bind setups, you can create a gaming experience that’s tailored to your preferences.

Pro Player Binds in CS2

Professional players use a wide variety of CS2 binds — for grenades, quick buys, jump-throws, voice commands, and even switching hands.

For example, s1mple is known to use binds for quick grenade usage and weapon view switching.

Example bind for switching hands:

bind "MOUSE4" "toggle cl_righthand 0 1"

Example bind for jump-throwing with a shot:

bind "SPACE" "+jump;-attack;-jump"

Learning and using the same commands as the pros can help simplify your gameplay and give you a competitive edge. Just find the ones that fit your playstyle.

Quick team messages:

How to Create Your Own Bind Config

To make things easier, you can create your own configuration file for binds. Follow these steps:

  • Open the folder: ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\game\csgo\cfg

  • Create a new file named autoexec.cfg

  • Paste your custom binds into this file

  • In your Steam launch options, add the following line: +exec autoexec.cfg

Now your binds will load automatically every time you launch CS2.

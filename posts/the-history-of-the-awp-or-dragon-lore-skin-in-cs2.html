The History of the AWP | Dragon Lore Skin in CS2

farmskins

AWP | Dragon Lore CS2 skin is perhaps the most iconic skin in the history of Counter-Strike. Its rarity, beautiful visuals, and legendary status among players have made it the dream of every gamer, especially among professionals and collectors. This skin is a true work of art in the world of in-game items.

Creation of the Skin and Its Visual Appeal

AWP | Dragon Lore first appeared in the game on July 1, 2014. This skin was created by Valve developers themselves and became part of the Cobblestone collection. Visually, it stands out with its golden coating with various shades, over which a red dragon is depicted breathing fire. This combination of colors and details makes the skin truly unique and desirable among players.

Since its introduction, the AWP | Dragon Lore has become a symbol of CS and a coveted item for many players. Its rarity and visual appeal have made it a cult skin, an integral part of the game's culture. For example, professional player PashaBiceps even got a tattoo of this skin, highlighting its influence on the esports community.

Popularity Among Professionals and the Community

Many streamers and YouTubers, including famous players like S1mple, have actively tried to craft or unbox the CS2 AWP | Dragon Lore from cases. Successful attempts at this turned into real events, and moments with this skin have been remembered by players for years. Particularly, there are many highlights featuring the AWP | Dragon Lore in matches from the 2015-2017 period when the skin's popularity peaked.

Owning this skin became not only a status symbol but also a source of pride. For instance, well-known player Dosia mentioned in one of his streams that he specifically bought the AWP | Dragon Lore to delight his teammates by dropping it for them during matches. No other skin, even rare ones like "Gungnir" or "Fade," evokes such excitement and respect among players.

Valve's Support and Ways to Obtain the Skin

Valve also acknowledged the significance of this skin by adding a sticker featuring the AWP | Dragon Lore in the CS20 sticker capsule, released in honor of the series' 20th anniversary. This was a unique case where a skin received such an honor, emphasizing its special place in the game.

However, getting your hands on the AWP | Dragon Lore is no easy task. In the past, it could be obtained after matches or through Cobblestone collection cases, but since then, the chances of acquiring it have become almost nonexistent. Today, there are a few ways to obtain this skin, all of which are quite difficult and expensive.

  • Opening Souvenir Cobblestone Cases: These cases once dropped for watching Major matches, but now their quantity is extremely limited. Opening one such case will cost around 40,000 rubles, and the chance of getting a AWP | Dragon Lore from them is less than one percent. However, if you're lucky, you might get a souvenir version of the skin, which can be worth several million rubles.

  • Crafting through the Trade-Up Contract system: This method involves exchanging 10 skins for one of higher quality. The most popular way is crafting through "Knight" skins, which cost around 150,000 rubles. You have a chance to get the "Dragon Lore," but even with a 10% chance, the cost does not guarantee a return.

  • Direct Purchase: This is the simplest yet most expensive way. Since the skin's release in 2014, its price has consistently increased. For example, in 2019, its value rose tenfold, reaching a million rubles for a "Factory New" version.

  • Opening Cases on Farmskins: Besides official methods, many players turn to opening cases on Farmskins. Farmskins offers the chance to win valuable skins, including the "Dragon Lore," for relatively small amounts of money.

Conclusion

The AWP | Dragon Lore is not just a CS2 skin; it is a symbol of CS2, representing prestige, rarity, and the history of the game. Its incredible popularity and high price make it a cult object that inspires millions of players worldwide. Today, the AWP | Dragon Lore remains one of the most sought-after items, and its value continues to rise, making it an even more valuable trophy in the world of CS2.

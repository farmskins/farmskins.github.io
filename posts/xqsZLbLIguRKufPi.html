Playing with the SG553 and AUG in CS2: Is Scoped Weaponry Worth Using?

farmskins

In most cases, CS2 players stick to the reliable classics — the AK-47 for terrorists and the M4A4 for counter-terrorists. It makes sense: both rifles are affordable and effective at any level of play. However, the CS2 arsenal also includes less popular but highly effective alternatives — the SG553 and AUG. These are scoped weapons that open up new tactical possibilities. In this article, we’ll explore whether using the SG553 and AUG in CS2 is a smart choice and if they can be considered worthy replacements for traditional rifles.

What Are the SG553 and AUG in CS2?

The SG553 in CS2 is an automatic rifle available to Terrorists. With its built-in scope, which increases the view by 3x and improves shooting accuracy while reducing recoil, it is excellent for controlling positions at various ranges.Although the SG553 (also known as the Krieg) was nerfed after a period of dominance, it remains deadly in the hands of a skilled player. 

The AUG in CS2 is an alternative version of the rifle for Counter-Terrorists. The weapon is also equipped with a scope and boasts the highest accuracy for the first shot in zoom among all assault rifles. The AUG is especially useful for holding positions at any range and performs well on open maps.

Playing with the SG553 in CS2

Playing with the CS2 SG553 is perfect for players who like to control engagements and prefer scoped shooting. The SG553 lets you confidently win long-range duels, and thanks to its solid rate of fire, it can also be used in close-quarters combat by players with fast reflexes.

However, there are some drawbacks: it’s more expensive than the AK-47, less reliable when firing from the hip, and has the slowest movement speed among all assault rifles. But what’s the better choice in CS2 — the SG553 or the AK-47? The answer is simple: it all depends on your gameplay and strategy. Try both options to see which one suits you better and helps you win more often.

Playing with the AUG in CS2

The AUG performs especially well on maps like Overpass, Nuke, and Mirage. Thanks to its built-in scope, the AUG allows effective control of long sightlines and chokepoints. Players most commonly use this rifle for defensive purposes.

The main advantage of the CS2 AUG is its ease of use, especially for beginners — just aim and shoot. But like any other rifle, it has its downsides: its price, especially in tough economic rounds, and its long reload time. So, AUG or M4A4 in CS2 — which should you choose? The M4A4 is more universal, but the AUG can provide a serious advantage when precision is key.

Is It Worth Buying the SG553 in CS2?

Should you buy the SG553 in CS2? If you need excellent zoom and know how to position yourself properly, the SG553 is your choice. This rifle performs well on maps with open spaces, where the AK-47 might not provide the necessary precision. The SG553 in CS2 is a rifle that can win rounds and shape your overall strategy.

Is It Worth Buying the AUG in CS2?

Should you pick up the AUG in CS2? If you often play as an anchor and are responsible for holding a bombsite, the AUG is a perfect fit. This rifle lets you control the battlefield and stop enemies before they get too close. The AUG requires patience and precision, not aggression. It’s a weapon for disciplined players who excel at timing and information play.

Alternative Weapons in CS2: When to Use the SG553 and AUG

The SG553 and AUG are alternative weapons in CS2 that are rarely seen in competitive play but can turn the tide of a round in the right hands. They are more expensive than standard rifles but offer advantages in precision and control. If you trust your aim and know how to maintain distance, give these rifles a try. Sometimes, an unconventional weapon choice can be the key to victory.

Conclusion

Playing with the SG553 or AUG will unlock entirely different tactical and gameplay abilities that you won’t develop with other assault rifles. These weapons require a different approach but offer different strengths in return. Try bringing some variety to your playstyle — scoped rifles might just open up new tactical horizons for you.

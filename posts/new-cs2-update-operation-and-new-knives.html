New CS2 Update: Operation and New Knives

farmskins

On July 12, Valve released an update for Counter-Strike 2. Information about this is published on the official shooter website. The patch size was about 600 MB.

List of changes:

  • Ancient: fixed clipping on A's boxes, now you can jump on them.

  • Vertigo: fixed clipping on the edges of floors on A, now player models do not fall down when colliding with the railing.

  • Changed camera shake during grenade throw.

In addition, Gabe Follower shared that a mention of missions appeared in the code of the recent CS2 update. This may hint at Valve's work on a future operation.

Also, a dataminer discovered an empty slot for the next rare item in CS2, most likely a knife or gloves.

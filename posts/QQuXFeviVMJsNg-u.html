From 800,000 to over a million people—that’s the average number of peak concurrent players in CS:GO. No wonder many of them want to stand out and show off in a sense. But how? The Hyper Beast skins feature a vibrant, psychedelic design with a monstrous creature and bold colors like pink, blue, and purple. M4A1-S | Hyper Beast

This skin for the CS2 M4A1-S showcases intricate artwork with swirling patterns and bold hues, primarily in shades of pink, blue, and purple. The design gives it a fantastical, almost surreal look. Introduced as part of the "Chroma 2" case, which was released in March 2016, it comes in various wear conditions, including Factory New, Minimal Wear, Field-Tested, Well-Worn, and Battle-Scarred. It’s often sought after by collectors and players who appreciate unique and flashy weapon skins.

Price: from $10.59 

Cases: Covert, Covertgloves, M4A4 & M4A1-S, Gamer, Rush, M4A1 Silence, Doppy.

AWP | Hyper Beast

This is a total banger in the CS skin scene! Dropped in the "Wildfire" case back in February 2016, this skin has become a staple for those who want to flex hard in-game. It’s got that trippy vibe that just screams “look at me!” Perfect for those clutch moments when you’re trying to flex your skills or style on your opponents. This isn’t just a skin, it’s a lifestyle.

Price: from $17.08 

Cases: Covert, AWP, Streets of Fight, Blaze, Gamer, Rush, Fast AWP, Doppy, Hissing snake, Recoil.

Nova | Hyper Beast

This skin features a crazy mix of colors—think bright pinks, blues, and purples—paired with a beastly design that looks like it came straight out of a fever dream. It’s perfect for flexing your style, even if you’re rolling with a shotgun. While the Nova isn’t the most popular choice for some players, the Hyper Beast skin CS2 gives it a whole new level of cool. It is a great way to show you’ve got personality.

Price: from $12.95 

Five-SeveN | Hyper Beast

This CS2 hyper beast skin is a visual feast. It’s like the pistol got hit by a paint explosion, and it’s ready to turn heads. Perfect for those players who want their pistol to stand out while they’re popping off. While the Five-SeveN might not be the go-to for everyone, the Hyper Beast skin gives it some serious style points. It’s all about expressing your personality while you frag out. 

Price: from $19.31

Cases: Covert, Top Guns

Kickstart Quests and Rewards in Fortnite Chapter 6 Season 2

farmskins

With the release of the new season in Fortnite Chapter 6, fresh quests have been introduced, allowing players to level up quickly and unlock Battle Pass rewards. Each completed quest grants 15,000 XP, and most of the tasks are quite simple, so it’s worth completing them as soon as possible.

How to Complete All Kickstart Quests

This season's Kickstart Fortnite quests are divided into two main themes: "Rob a Vault" and "Crime Spree." These quests are related to the new criminal world mechanics and help players get acquainted with the updated gameplay system.

"Rob a Vault" Quests and How to Complete Them

  1. Visit Crime City, Lonewolf Lair, Seaport City, or Masked Meadows There are four key locations on the map that need to be explored. You don’t have to visit them in a single match, so you can complete this task at your own pace.

  2. Collect Thermite from Outlaw Vending Machines or Go Bags Thermite is a tool for breaking into vaults. It can be found in special vending machines or in Go Bags, which are similar to regular chests.

  3. Apply Thermite to a Bank Vault, Armored Transport, or Armored Train Once you have Thermite, apply it to one of three objects: a Bank Vault, an Armored Transport, or an Armored Train. Bank Vaults are stationary, while transport and trains move across the map.

  4. Damage Weak Points on the Vault During the Break-In During the break-in, glowing areas will appear on the vault’s surface. Attack these areas to speed up the process and complete the quest.

  5. Stage 1 — Collect Dill Bit This special currency can be found in bank vaults, armored transports, or armored trains.

  6. Stage 2 — Use the Dill Bit at the Black Market There are three Black Markets on the Fortnite map. Use the Dill Bit to purchase an item and complete the task.

"Crime Spree" Quests and How to Complete Them

  1. Mine a Gold Vein or Dive into the Gold River These locations are found on the west side of the map and are easy to spot due to the shining gold deposits.

  2. Purchase an Item from Each Black Market You will need three Dill Bits for this task. Visit all three Black Markets and buy at least one item at each.

  3. Find a Falcon Eye Sniper or Plasma Burst Laser in Three Different Matches These weapons can be found in loot chests. To complete the quest, you must find them in three separate matches.

  4. Restore 400 HP or Shields Using Med-Mist Grenades, Gold Splash, or Chug Jugs You can use any of the listed items. Progress is saved between matches.

  5. Eliminate Three Players with Legendary or Mythic Weapons Such Fortnite weapons are most commonly found in bank vaults, armored transports, or armored trains. This quest can be completed across multiple matches.

Why Complete Kickstart Quests?

Kickstart quests in Fortnite are a quick way to gain XP at the start of the season and unlock Battle Pass rewards. Not only do they speed up leveling, but they also help you master new game mechanics, such as heists, using thermite, and trading at the Black Market. Completing these quests will give you an advantage in matches and make the gameplay even more exciting.

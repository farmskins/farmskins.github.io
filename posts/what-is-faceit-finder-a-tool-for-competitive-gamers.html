FACEIT Finder can help with this — a tool that displays FACEIT stats and Steam profiles side by side, helping you find a FACEIT account using a Steam URL or other information. In this article, we will explore the main features of FACEIT Finder and its key benefits for competitive gaming.

What is FACEIT Finder?

FACEIT Finder is an innovative tool that provides a thorough analysis of individual players, allowing easy comparison of their performance. With its help, users can track their stats and check real-time game data, such as Counter-Strike 2 (CS2), including FACEIT ELO, headshot percentage, HLTV rating, and kill/death ratio. By entering a Steam ID into the corresponding field and clicking the "Lookup" button, players can quickly access detailed information about their account, including account creation date, status, total matches played, achievements, banned friends, and much more.

Key Features of FACEIT Finder

Detailed FACEIT Stats

  • Account Status: Check the current status of the account.
  • Creation Date: Discover when the account was created.
  • Achievements: View a list of achievements earned by the player.
  • Banned Friends: Information on whether the player's friends have been banned.
  • Total Hours Played: Find out the total hours a player has spent in CS2.

Performance Tracking

  • ELO: Track the player's ELO rating.
  • Headshot Percentage: Check the percentage of headshots made by the player.
  • HLTV Rating: View the player's rating on the HLTV scale.
  • Kill/Death Ratio: See the player's kill/death ratio.

Steam Profile Details

  • Profile Overview: Access the player's profile name, level, badge collection, and rewards.
  • Groups and Friends: View the player's groups and friends list.
  • Game Playlist: Review the list of games and account identifiers.
  • Hours Played: Check the total hours spent in various games.
  • Bans and Restrictions: Learn if the player has any bans or restrictions on their account.

Player Level Display

  • ELO Points: Shows the player's level based on their ELO points.
  • ELO Points Table: A detailed table that helps understand how ELO points translate into ranks.

Performance Comparison

  • Compare Players: Enter the Steam ID or full URL of two players to compare their performance.
  • Teammate Comparison: View and compare teammate stats.

Benefits of Using FACEIT Finder

  • Real-time Player Matching
    FACEIT Finder makes it easy to find people for team games, matching players based on their skills, playstyle, and goals. This system quickly finds suitable partners, allowing you to start a game faster.

  • Accelerating the Learning Process
    Playing with compatible teammates can significantly speed up the learning process. Sharing tips and strategies helps the entire team develop faster.

  • Eliminating Hackers and Cheaters
    FACEIT Finder provides verified information about players, allowing you to determine if a player is honest or using cheats. This helps create reliable teams.

  • Variety of Supported Games
    In addition to CS2, FACEIT Finder supports games like PUBG, League of Legends, DOTA 2, and TF2. This ensures a fair and enjoyable gaming experience across different games.

  • ELO Ranking System
    FACEIT Finder helps users better understand the ELO ranking system on FACEIT. Knowing your opponent's ELO level before a match can give you a strategic advantage.

ELO Ranking System

On FACEIT, the ELO ranking system categorizes players based on their skill level. The ranks range from Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 10 (Legendary). Here are some of them:

  • Level 1: 1 - 800 (Beginner)

  • Level 2: 801 - 950 (Beginner)

  • Level 3: 951 - 1100 (Beginner)

  • Level 4: 1101 - 1250 (Amateur)

  • Level 5: 1251 - 1400 (Semi-Pro)

  • Level 6: 1401 - 1550 (Pro)

  • Level 7: 1551 - 1700 (Elite)

  • Level 8: 1701 - 1850 (Master)

  • Level 9: 1851 - 2000 (Grandmaster)

  • Level 10: 2001+ (Legendary)

Winning a match usually adds 25 points to ELO, while losing results in a point loss. Games with more experienced opponents raise the stakes, making the competition intense and rewarding high performance.

Tips for Improving Your FACEIT Ranking

  • Master Maps: Learn map layouts and key points to develop strategies.

  • Compete with High-Rank Players: Playing with strong opponents helps you develop faster.

  • Avoid Shortcuts: Don't use cheating methods to boost your rank.

  • Refresh and Recharge: Take breaks to avoid tilt and keep your mind fresh.

  • Analyze Your Matches: After each game, review your actions and mistakes.

  • Teamwork: Communication is key to successful team play.

  • Regular Practice: Consistent practice is essential to maintaining and improving your skills.

Conclusion

FACEIT Finder is an invaluable tool for competitive gamers looking to improve their performance and enjoy fair play. Detailed stats, real-time matchmaking, and verified player information help you reach new heights on FACEIT. Regardless of your level, using CS2 FACEIT Finder can significantly enhance your gaming experience and help you unlock your potential in esports.

All About Service Medals in CS2: A Complete Guide

farmskins

Service Medals in CS2 are unique rewards that showcase a player's dedication and achievements in the game over a specific year. They represent the time and effort invested in the game and can become not only a source of pride but also a potential source of income.

What Are Service Medals?

Service Medals are awards given for active participation in the game during a specific calendar year. To earn these medals, players must accumulate experience points (XP) each week and reach certain levels. The medal can either be obtained or upgraded within the current year, after which it is no longer possible to acquire the previous version.

These CS2 medals are displayed in the player’s Steam inventory and mini-profile alongside other awards, such as medals for participation in operations or years of service. It is important to note that service medals cannot be transferred or sold directly through Steam, but accounts with these medals may be valued higher.

Mechanics of Earning Medals

Service Medals were introduced in mid-2015 along with the ranking system. There are a total of 40 ranks, and upon reaching rank 40, a player has the opportunity to earn a Service Medal or upgrade it if they already possess the medal for the current year.

Initially, in 2015, there were only two levels of medals: blue and pink. However, over time, the system became more complex: in 2016 and 2017, up to seven levels of medals were added, with the highest levels (black medals) being so rare that no player managed to obtain them.

Since 2018, the number of medal levels has been reduced to six, and this structure has remained unchanged. The colors of the medals have also stayed the same since 2018: white, green, blue, purple, pink, and red. The red medal is the rarest and most valuable, as obtaining it requires reaching rank 40 six times in a single year.

How to Earn Medals Faster?

Medals in CS2 can be earned both honestly and through various tricks. For example, to speed up the process, players can use XP boosters obtained from previous operations, such as "Broken Fang" or "Shattered Web." Each booster grants 5,000 experience points, which is equivalent to one full rank.

The honest way to obtain the red medal is to play a lot and use all available XP boosters. However, some players have tried to obtain medals through unfair means, using so-called "account farms." In such cases, a panel would be launched that automatically added all accounts to one match, collecting XP while kicking out regular players. This method led to waves of account bans.

Where to Sell an Account with Medals?

While the medals themselves are not transferable or sellable, accounts with rare and high-level medals can be worth a significant amount of money. The more rare medals an account has, the higher its value. On average, a red medal could be priced at around $300 or more, depending on the account’s age, the presence of other games and items, and whether or not it has any bans.

Conclusion

Service Medals in CS2 are not just decorative items in your inventory but symbols of persistence and dedication to the game. Medals can be earned both through honest gameplay and through various strategies, but it's important to remember that dishonest methods may lead to account bans. Medals demonstrate your contribution to the game and are a valuable addition to your CS2 profile.

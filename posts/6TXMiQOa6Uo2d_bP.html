What Did the First Trailer Show?

The debut trailer impressed with its graphics and attention to detail. Vice City has come to life in the new generation: beaches filled with sunbathers, modern skyscrapers, cars with detailed damage, and even alligators in the swamps. The city’s atmosphere is enhanced by rich colors and dynamic weather changes, creating the feeling of a living world.

One of the most intriguing aspects is the possible reverse chronology of events. Sharp-eyed fans noticed that the main character, Lucia, is in prison at the beginning of the GTA 6 trailer but is later seen committing robberies with Jason. The electronic ankle bracelet on her leg supports the theory that some scenes in the trailer might be shown in reverse order.

Gameplay and Physics Details

The trailer also showcased significant changes in NPC behavior and overall game physics. Pedestrians now have unique activities, from exercising to applying sunscreen. The world is more detailed: characters leave footprints in the sand, stir up dust, and their clothing realistically wrinkles as they move.

Vehicle and environmental physics have also reached a new level. Cars take visible damage, tires deform, and planes leave condensation trails. Water reacts to wind, and objects in the world are destructible. Developers have paid special attention to animations and the realism of character interactions with their surroundings.

Who Are the Main Characters?

The trailer confirmed that the key characters in the game are Lucia and Jason. Lucia is the first-ever female protagonist in a GTA game, and she clearly has a criminal past. In the heist scenes, she appears experienced in dangerous situations. Less is known about Jason, but it’s evident that he is her partner.

A popular theory suggests that Lucia and Jason are siblings. Sources claim that the story will revolve around two estranged relatives who end up on opposite sides of the law. While this has not been officially confirmed, Rockstar is known for surprising players with unexpected plot twists.

Will There Be Sex in GTA 6?

In recent years, Rockstar has changed its approach to portraying relationships in its games. In Red Dead Redemption 2, Arthur Morgan remained loyal to his former love, while John Marston was portrayed as a devoted husband. Therefore, in GTA 6, Lucia and Jason’s relationship will likely follow a Bonnie and Clyde-style narrative without random flings or betrayals.

Insider GTA 6 Leaks and Rumors

Journalist Jason Schreier stated that GTA 6 could become the highest-grossing game in history, with its online mode generating revenue for years. Insiders claim that Rockstar has focused not only on graphics but also on deep world interaction. For example, NPCs might call the police if they notice a body in a car trunk. Additionally, destruction physics will be enhanced, and the main character will be able to store weapons and equipment in their vehicle.

Rumors suggest that the second trailer is already completed and could be released in the first quarter of 2025, further fueling excitement for the game.

Release Date and Possible Delays

Rockstar has officially confirmed that GTA 6 release date will be in Fall 2025 on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S. There is no information about a PC version yet, but based on previous releases, a PC port might not arrive until at least 2026.

Some sources claim the release date is set for September 17, 2025—the same day GTA 5 was released in 2013. While this has not been officially confirmed, such a move would be symbolic.

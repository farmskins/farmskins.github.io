How to Import Crosshairs in CS2

Importing CS2 crosshairs is quick and easy. First, launch the game and head to the settings menu by clicking the gear icon on the main screen. From there, go to the “Game” tab and find the “Crosshair” section. You’ll see an option to “Share or Import” crosshair codes. Click it, paste the code you’ve copied from a pro player, and hit the “Import” button. Boom—your new crosshair is ready to go! You can also tweak it further in the same menu to adjust things like size, color, and style to suit your personal preferences

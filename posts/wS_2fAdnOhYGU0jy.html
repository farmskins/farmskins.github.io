AWP | Dragon Lore

We can’t kick off this list in a better way than by spotlighting the legendary AWP | Dragon Lore. Whether you’ve logged 1 hour or 10,000 hours in CS, there’s a solid chance you’ve heard of it. The CS2 AWP | Dragon Lore is the ultimate skin, combining insane rarity, a god-tier design, and a price tag that makes it the pinnacle of any collection. 

Price: from $4,074 

Cases: Gungnir, Exclusive, AWP, Lore.

M4A4 | Dragon King

This CS2 skin Dragon King offers players a chance to unleash some serious style and swag on the battlefield. Released as part of the “Danger Zone” collection in December 2018, this skin features a stunning dragon design that embodies the power and ferocity of its namesake, making it a must-have for any serious player looking to add a little extra flare to their gameplay.

Price: from $5.92 

Cases: M4A4 & M4A1-S, Mushu

Dual Berettas | Dualing Dragons

The last skin might have been a steal, but we can take it even lower! Check out this sleek pair of Dual Berettas, featuring a stunning wooden handle and a dragon intricately etched into the steel. Not only are they affordable, but they also pack some serious style, which is why they’ve secured a spot on our list of the best dragon skins.

Price: from $0.31 

P90 | Emerald Dragon

The P90 | Emerald Dragon is a stunning, visually appealing CS2 skin that combines striking design and unique aesthetic. Part of the “Chroma 2” collection, this skin brings together the P90’s reputation as a versatile submachine gun and a beautiful, intricate dragon motif that makes it stand out in any match. Whether you’re an eco warrior or just want to look good while fragging, the Emerald Dragon is a solid choice for your loadout!

Price: from $77.80 

SSG 08 | Dragonfire

Looking to frag in style during a low buy round? This stunning scout skin has got you covered! With a fiery red and orange background, it showcases a fierce blue and black dragon CS2 complete with menacing sharp teeth, making it a perfect choice for those looking to make a statement on the battlefield. It’s perfect for players looking to make an impact while keeping things budget-friendly.

Price: from $9.26 

Cases: Covertknives, Covert, Covertgloves, Blaze, StatTrak Abuse, The Boss, Gunsmith.

AWP | Wildfire

Look at this fiery and bold skin that has earned its place in the hearts of CS players. Released as part of the "Chroma 3" collection, this skin features an intense design that’s perfect for players who want to light up the battlefield. Compared to other high-tier AWP skins CS2, the Wildfire is relatively affordable. This makes it a popular choice for players who want a sick skin without dropping a ton of cash.

Price: from $27.31 

Cases: Gungnir, Exclusive, Covertknives, Covert, Covertgloves, AWP, Kingdom, MK, StatTrak Abuse, The Boss, Gunsmith, Fast AWP, Ez Covert, Hissing snake, Lore.

SG 553 | Dragon Tech

The SG 553 | Dragon Tech, created by Aleks36 in early July 2022, is a custom-painted dragon skin CS2 with a unique blend of design elements that merge traditional oriental aesthetics with a cyberpunk vibe, making it a standout choice for players who want to make a statement in the game. This skin is often considered a collector’s dream, appealing to those who appreciate unique and artistic skins in their inventory.

Price: from $0.29 

Cases: Recoil

Glock-18 | Dragon Tattoo

Released as part of the "The Rising Sun" collection, this skin features a bold dragon design that enhances the iconic pistol's appearance and reflects a blend of cultural themes. While it’s not as frequently featured as some of the more high-tier skins, it has made appearances among skilled players like CS2 m0NESY, sh1ro, FalleN, s1mple, and Niko.

Price: from $81.21 

P2000 | Imperial Dragon

It is a must-have for gamers who want to bring some serious style to their sidearm. Decked out in vibrant reds and golds, this skin features an epic dragon design that blends traditional art with a modern edge. Plus, it’s wallet-friendly, making it a solid choice for eco rounds. Whether you’re flexing on your squad or building your collection, the Imperial Dragon CSGO is sure to make your gameplay lit.

Price: from $2.46

Desert Eagle | Kumicho Dragon

This skin features a fierce dragon design in vibrant red and black, inspired by traditional Japanese art, giving it a unique and eye-catching look. With its striking details and bold colors, the Kumicho Dragon isn’t just about style—it’s about delivering serious firepower with flair. Whether you’re clutching an eco round or going for a big play, this Deagle is sure to turn heads and strike fear into your enemies.

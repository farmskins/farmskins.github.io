Souvenir Skins in CS2

Souvenir skins in CS2 are a special type of weapon skin that drops during major tournaments—such as Majors—and are linked to specific matches. These skins have distinctive markings: they feature stickers from the participating teams and the autograph of the round’s MVP. So, if you’re wondering what is a CS2 souvenir, it's essentially a commemorative tournament item directly tied to esports events.

How to Get Souvenir Skins in CS2

Nowadays, souvenir drops in CS2 are no longer handed out randomly like in the past. Instead, players can receive a CS2 souvenir item by watching official matches on the in-game platform or via Steam.tv—provided they have an active Viewer Pass. By watching matches, especially those where the player participates in Pick'Em challenges or makes predictions, it's possible to receive souvenir cases containing CS2 souvenir skins.

Here’s what you need to do:

  • Purchase a Viewer Pass for the tournament.

  • Activate it and link it to your Steam account.

  • Watch tournament matches.

  • Receive a CS2 souvenir drop during key match moments.

Why Are Souvenir Skins Valuable in CS2?

The main purpose of these skins is for collecting and investing. While they don't enhance weapon performance, they:

  • Are tied to historic matches and iconic CS2 moments.

  • Are time-limited and rare, especially those from high-profile matches featuring famous players.

  • Can significantly increase in value over time.

So, why are CS2 souvenir skins important? They let players preserve a piece of tournament history, show their dedication to esports, and potentially profit from future market value increases.

Differences Between Souvenir Skins and Regular Skins

  • Origin: Though they have float values, souvenir skins CS2 don’t come from cases or trade-up contracts.

  • Exclusive stickers: Only souvenir skins feature both team stickers and player autographs on a single weapon.

  • Stickers are locked: The stickers on a CS2 souvenir item cannot be removed or replaced, making each skin one-of-a-kind.

Popular Souvenir Skins

Some CS2 souvenirs are especially sought-after by collectors:

  • AWP | Dragon Lore (Souvenir) – The most iconic tournament skin in CS2, known for its high price.

  • M4A1-S | Knight (Souvenir)

  • AK-47 | Gold Arabesque (Souvenir)

These skins can sell for tens of thousands of dollars, particularly if they were dropped during the finals of major tournaments.

Conclusion

Now you know what CS2 souvenir skins are, why they matter, and how to get them. These items are more than just attractive weapon skins—they’re actual artifacts of Counter-Strike history. Keep up with tournaments, take part in events, and maybe you’ll get lucky and score one of these rare CS2 souvenir items yourself.

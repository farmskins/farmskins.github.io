Why Are CS2 Knives So Expensive?

farmskins

Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) is known for its wide variety of skins, among which knives stand out for their high prices. While many skins can be purchased for a few hundred dollars, knife prices start at $200 and can reach thousands. This article explains why CS2 knives are so expensive and what makes them so desirable.

Variety of Knives in CS2

First, it's important to understand the types of knives available. In CS2, players start each round with a knife. The game features two standard knives: one used by the attacking side and the other by the defending side. The design of the counter-terrorist CS2 knife is based on the Eickhorn Recondo IV model, adding realism and appeal.

Besides the standard knives, the game offers nearly two dozen cosmetic variations. These knives come in unique shapes and designs, from the bayonet and karambit to the butterfly knife and many others. These cosmetic variations are the objects of desire for many players.

Reasons for the High Cost of Knives in CS2

Popularity and Iconic Status

Knives in CS2 have become iconic items. Developers have put significant effort into creating them, making them visually appealing and equipping them with various animations. For example, the spinning motion of the butterfly knife or the swift movements with the paracord knife. These details make the knives unique and desirable among players.

Difficulty of Acquisition

Knives are among the rarest items obtainable from cases in CS2. The probability of getting a knife when opening a case is just over 0.2%. This means players have to open hundreds, sometimes thousands, of cases to get the desired knife. In monetary terms, this can mean spending thousands of dollars.

No Crafting Option

Unlike other items, knives in CS2 cannot be created by merging or combining other items. The crafting system does not apply to knives, making them even rarer and more desirable. The lack of a crafting option increases their market value, as the only ways to obtain a knife are through case openings, trading, or purchasing from other players.

Case Drops

One of the main reasons for the high cost of knives is their rarity in case drops. The chance of getting a knife is just over 0.2%, meaning you need to open around four hundred cases for one knife. This makes the process of obtaining a knife costly and time-consuming.

Considering the various color patterns and the presence of kill counters (StatTrak), the cost and difficulty of obtaining knives increase significantly. For example, obtaining a Karambit Crimson Web might require opening up to five thousand cases. Getting a version with a kill counter (StatTrak) is even more challenging: the probability of getting a StatTrak skin is only 10%. This means that to get a knife with this feature, you need to open around fifty thousand cases, which can cost over $120,000.

Crafting Knives in CS2

Another important feature of knives in CS2 is the impossibility of creating them through the crafting system. While many other items can be made by merging several less valuable items, knives cannot be crafted. This makes them even rarer and more valuable to collectors and players.

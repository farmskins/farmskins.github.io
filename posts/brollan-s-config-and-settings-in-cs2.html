Brollan's Config and Settings in CS2

Ludvig "Brollan" Brolin is a beast in the CS2 arena. This Swedish rifler is known for his sick aim and strategic play, making him a force to be reckoned with since he burst onto the scene in 2017. With his razor-sharp skills, Brollan has earned a spot in the top-20 players twice in his career. He initially joined MOUZ on loan in January, 2024. His impressive performance helped MOUZ achieve the No.1 spot in the world rankings for the first time in the organization’s history.  Let’s break down Brollan’s CS2 config and see how you can level up your game by learning from one of the best.

Brollan’s Config in CS2

If you're serious about stepping up your CS2 game, Brollan’s config is the blueprint. This setup isn't just about looking cool—it's about optimizing every aspect of gameplay for maximum impact. You can download his config file right here, giving you a direct line to the settings that power his pro-level play.

Brollan’s Sensitivity in CS2

Brollan’s sensitivity settings are all about control and precision. He rocks a 800 DPI with a sensitivity of 1.3. This combination strikes a balance between speed and accuracy, allowing him to make precise shots in high-pressure situations. 

  • DPI: 800

●  Sensitivity: 1.3

●  eDPI: 1040

●  Hz: 2000

●  Zoom Sensitivity: 1

●  Windows sensitivity: 6

Brollan’s Crosshair in CS2

When it comes to crosshairs, Brollan keeps it simple and effective. His choice of a cyan crosshair is all about visibility and precision. This setup is easy to spot against any background, helping him stay locked on targets.

To make the same crosshair, copy and paste the following text into the game settings:

CSGO-FnJLE-pUbZP-vnNr4-OEcBH-2zpxA

Brollan’s Graphics Settings in CS2 

Brollan opts for a 1280x960 resolution with a 4:3 aspect ratio stretched to help him spot enemies faster. Combine that with low graphic settings to boost FPS, and you've got a setup designed to give you every advantage.

●  Resolution: 1280x960

●  Aspect Ratio: 4:3

●  Scaling Mode: Stretched

●  Brightness: 110%

●  Display Mode: Fullscreen

●  Boost Player Contrast: Enabled

●  V-Sync: Disabled

●  Multisampling Anti-Aliasing Mode: 8x MSAA

●  Global Shadow Quality: Low

●  Model / Texture Detail: Medium

●  Texture Filtering Mode: Bilinear

●  Shader Detail: Low

Brollan’s Viewmodel Settings in CS2

Brollan’s viewmodel settings are optimized for a clear field of vision, keeping distractions at a minimum and his focus on the enemy. Check out his setup below to keep your hands out of the way and your eyes on the prize:

viewmodel_fov 68; viewmodel_offset_x 2.5; viewmodel_offset_y 0; viewmodel_offset_z -1.5; viewmodel_presetpos 2;

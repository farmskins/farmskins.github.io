Biguzera extends contract with paiN Gaming

farmskins

Esports organization paiN Gaming has announced the extension of its contract with Rodrigo "biguzera" Bittencourt, the captain of its Counter-Strike 2 roster. The news was shared via the club’s official social media channels.

Details of the new contract have not been disclosed. The Brazilian player has been representing paiN since February 2019 — during this time, biguzera and paiN have secured multiple tournament victories and consistently participated in major CS2 events.

paiN Gaming recently defeated FURIA Esports in the quarterfinals of BLAST.tv Austin Major 2025 in CS2. The series ended 2:1 — 6:13 on Nuke, 13:8 on Anubis, and 16:12 on Inferno. Soares’s team advanced to the semifinals, where they will face the winner of FaZe Clan vs The MongolZ.

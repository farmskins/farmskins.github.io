Skins for M4A4 and M4A1-S

CS2 is famous for its wide variety of skins, and the M4A4 and M4A1-S rifles are no exception, with their own unique designs. The most famous skins for the M4A4 include Griffin, Tooth Fairy, and Asiimov, while the most popular M4A1-S skins are Flashback, Briefing, and Basilisk. Today, however, we’ll take a look at some lesser-known, but equally interesting skins for the M4A4 and M4A1-S.

M4A4 vs M4A1-S: What’s the Difference?

Now, let's move on to performance. Which rifle is better in CS2? It's difficult to say, as it all depends on individual player preferences, playstyle, and positions on the map. When using the M4A4, professionals often shoot from the hip, reserving their ammo for close combat situations. On long-range distances, burst fire is more effective than single shots.

The M4A1-S, equipped with a silencer, has higher first-shot accuracy, making it an ideal weapon for quick maneuvers. The M4A1-S excels at long distances, and short bursts are the most effective firing option for it.

Damage and Armor Penetration

In terms of damage, the M4A1-S boasts a base damage of 38, while the M4A4 deals 33 damage. Both rifles have identical armor penetration at 70%.

Magazine Capacity

  • M4A4 – 30 rounds per magazine, with a total of 90 rounds in reserve.

  • M4A1-S – 20 rounds per magazine, with 80 rounds in reserve.

If your playstyle involves aggressive tactics and a high rate of fire, the M4A4 in CS2 will be your best choice. On the other hand, if you prefer precise shots and efficient ammo usage, the M4A1-S is your go-to weapon.

Advantages of the Silencer

Using the M4A1-S with a silencer greatly improves accuracy. In testing, both rifles placed their first shots with the same spread of 0.005. However, the second-shot deviation for the M4A1-S was 0.011, while the M4A1-S with a silencer had a deviation of 0.008. The lower the value, the more accurate the weapon.

Simplicity and Cost

The M4A4 is often compared to the AK-47 due to its similar recoil pattern, although opinions on this vary. The price is also a factor: the M4A4 costs $3000, making it the most expensive assault rifle in the updated version of Counter-Strike. The M4A1-S is $100 cheaper, which is significant, even for professional players.

M4A4 vs M4A1-S: Which Should You Choose?

If you're a beginner, the M4A1-S with its easy recoil control is the optimal choice. However, if you're aiming to become the MVP, the M4A4, with its larger magazine and greater tactical possibilities, is the way to go. Ultimately, it’s important to immerse yourself in the game, try out both rifles, and find the one that suits your unique playstyle. Additionally, M4A4 and M4A1-S skins can help you stand out on the battlefield.

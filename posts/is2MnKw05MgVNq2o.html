AK-47 | Case Hardened

The AK-47 | Case Hardened is known for its beautiful, randomized patterns of blue, gold, and purple shades, which make each CS:GO skin truly unique. The vibrant colors covering the weapon make it a must-have for any collector.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $330  

Cases: Drops from: Gungnir, Exclusive, Classified, AK-47 Lucky AK-47, Lotus 

AWP | Lightning Strike

The CS:GO AWP | Lightning Strike features a sleek purple base with a lightning bolt streaking across it, making it both striking and menacing on the battlefield.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $300-$600  

Cases: Drops from Gungnir, Exclusive, Covert, Covertgloves, CS2 AWP, Mushu, Fast AWP, Hissing Snake 

Desert Eagle | Hypnotic

The Desert Eagle | Hypnotic comes with a mesmerizing black-and-white spiral pattern that truly lives up to its name. Its design is simple yet captivating, making it a popular choice among Deagle enthusiasts.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $100-$200  

Cases: Drops from: Desert Eagle

Glock-18 | Fade

The Glock-18 | Fade is instantly recognizable with its gradient fade of purple, pink, and orange, giving it a modern and flashy look. This skin in CS:GO is perfect for those who want to stand out.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $200-$350  

Cases: Drops from: Gungnir, Exclusive, Restricted, Top Guns, Astarot, Gunsmith 

M4A1-S | Dark Water

The CS:GO M4A1-S | Dark Water is shrouded in mystery with its dark and murky pattern, creating a sleek and tactical appearance that’s perfect for stealthy players.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $100-$250  

Cases: Drops from: Restricted,  M4A4 & M4A1-S, M4A1 Silence 

USP-S | Dark Water

The USP-S | Dark Water, like the M4A1-S in CS:GO counterpart, features the same dark and enigmatic pattern, making it an excellent pairing for those who value style and stealth.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $70-$150  

Cases: Drops from: https://farmskins.com/classic-restricted 

AUG | Wings

The AUG | Wings boasts a delicate feathered pattern in shades of gray, giving it an elegant and unique appearance that stands out from other AUG skins.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $50-$120  

Cases: Drops from: AUG Wings

SG 553 | Ultraviolet

The SG 553 | Ultraviolet is a simple yet impactful design, featuring a solid black base with deep purple accents, making it a subtle but powerful choice.

Release Date: August 14, 2013

Price: $60-$130  

Cases: Drops from: SG 553 Ultraviolet 

MP7 | Skulls

The MP7 | Skulls skin features a dark, intimidating design with an array of skulls etched all over the body of the gun. The base color is primarily black, providing a stark contrast to the detailed white skull patterns that cover the CS:GO weapon. The skulls are depicted in various sizes and orientations, creating a chaotic yet visually striking look, making it a popular choice for players who want a menacing aesthetic.

Release Date: February, 2014

Price: $3 - $10  

Cases: Drops from: MP7 Skulls 

With these iconic skins, your CS2 inventory is sure to stand out. From the vibrant Fade to the mysterious Dark Water, there’s something here for every type of player. Grab one of these skins and make every match a little more stylish!

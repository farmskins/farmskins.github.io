The History of Perfect World

Perfect World is a dominant player in the Chinese gaming market, having achieved success through strategic cooperation with Valve. In 2017, the company was granted the rights to distribute CS in China, launching the game first in closed beta and later in open beta. This launch was accompanied by unique features tailored to the Chinese audience, helping the game adapt to local market demands.

Perfect World played a crucial role in localizing the game, providing players with a special launcher, identity verification system, and unique in-game content, which includes exclusive items inspired by Chinese culture. These steps allowed the company to strengthen its position and create favorable conditions for Chinese players to participate in international esports tournaments.

Key Achievements and Contributions

Since its debut in the esports scene, Perfect World has actively organized and supported major tournaments, contributing to the development of competitive gaming in Asia. The company has made a substantial impact on popularizing esports through events such as:

  • CS:GO Asia Championships 2018: With a prize pool of $300,000, this event marked Perfect World’s entry onto the international scene. It attracted top teams from around the world and served as a platform for future tournaments.

  • CS:GO Asia Championships 2019: With an increased prize pool of $500,000, this tournament reaffirmed the company’s commitment to developing esports in Asia and attracted even more top-tier teams.

  • CS Asia Championships 2023: Continuing its tradition of large-scale tournaments, Perfect World hosted an event with a $500,000 prize pool, which saw FaZe Clan emerge victorious.

In addition, the company has paid great attention to the development of the regional esports scene by organizing qualifiers and local events, which provide new talent with the opportunity to showcase their skills on the international stage.

Perfect World Shanghai Major 2024

The upcoming Perfect World Shanghai Major 2024 will be a landmark event for the entire Counter-Strike community. It will be the first Major held in Asia, and its significance goes far beyond a single tournament. Perfect World will once again emphasize its dedication to the development of esports in the region by offering unique opportunities for players and fans alike.

The tournament promises to gather the best teams from around the world, and its prize pool is expected to be one of the largest in CS2 history. The success of this event could usher in a new era of tournament organization in Asia, opening up new horizons for the region and attracting more investments and attention from the global community.

Perfect World’s Features and Innovations

Perfect World has introduced several unique features and innovations for the Chinese CS market, setting their version of the game apart:

  • Specialized Launcher: Designed to integrate with the Chinese platform, it provides localized features and ensures convenience for Chinese players.

  • Identity Verification Process: To comply with Chinese laws, players must undergo identity verification, ensuring safety and legality within the gaming environment.

  • Exclusive In-Game Content: Perfect World released collectible stickers and graffiti inspired by Chinese culture, which have become popular among both players and collectors.

  • Localized Servers: Dedicated servers for Chinese players ensure compliance with local laws and provide the best gaming experience.

Differences from the Global Version of CS2

While similar to the global version of CS2, the Perfect World client has several key differences:

  • Censorship Adjustments: Visual elements like blood and skull symbols have been altered or removed to comply with Chinese censorship laws.

  • Localized Interface: Achievements and commands have been localized for Chinese players, making the game more user-friendly for the local audience.

  • Limited Cross-Regional Play: Chinese players cannot join global servers, creating a more isolated gaming environment.

Future Prospects

The successful hosting of the Perfect World Shanghai Major 2024 could significantly reshape the future of Counter-Strike in Asia. This Major will serve as a starting point for new tournaments, both locally and internationally, and open up new opportunities for Asian players and teams. Perfect World continues to push boundaries, paving the way for further growth in the region's esports scene.

The Perfect World Shanghai Major 2024 will not only be a historic event for Asia, but also a significant step in the development of the global esports scene.
